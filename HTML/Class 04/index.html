Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my fav.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1tb butter
  • 2tb cream

Method!

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat.
  2. gently mix the egg and cream in a bowl.
  3. once butter has melted add eggs and cream.
  4. using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 secs.
  5. when the eggs are still moist remove from heat.
